About Us

Policy Monster is made by a group of Computer Science students who want to help people make informed data security decisions by translating privacy policies into a easy-to-read format. We believe that everyone deserves to know how their data is being used and what exactly they are agreeing to when they check the box that says "I have read and agree to the Terms and Conditions."
