Our Mission

We know that almost no one is reading privacy policies or terms of service, and we don't blame anyone for that. No one has the time! But these documents contain important information about how the service will use your personal information. We believe everyone has the right to be able to make informed decisions about their online presence and data, and our mission is to make these decisions easier and faster.

How it Works

Policy Monster makes it fast and easy to find the privacy policy or ToS of a website, no downloading files or goose-chasing necessary. Simply enter the URL and Policy Monster will automatically locate the policy and get right to work. You will be automatically redirected to the results page.
